Facts About Earth

  • Radius: 3959 miles
  • Fifth largest planet in the solar system
  • Water covers 70% of the surface
  • Not a perfect sphere; it is wider around its middle
  • Spinning at just over 1,000 miles per hour
  • Moving around the sun at 67,000 miles per hour
  • Coldest place on Earth is in Antarctica
  • Deepest point on the ocean floor is the Mariana Trench
